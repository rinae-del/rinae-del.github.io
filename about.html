The window:

I was peeking through the curtain, looking out the window. I can only decscribe what I saw as beauty. It's raining the streets are empty, change is approaching. TIK, TIK , TIK as the clock on the wall remind me that time is running out. As I write, the world is facing a threat, people are in fear. Freedom has been taken away. People have lost jobs, businesses are down and we're under home arrest. living in times like this sure feels terrible, but not for everyone.

Tip of my fingers:

This lockdown doesn't feel like much to me. All I need is not in malls or gatherings but right at the tip of my fingers, the computer. If you start messing around with various softwares, you switch your computer from being just a storage box to a toolbox. Once you identify useful computer tools and learn to manipulate them, you finally understand why such a machine have such value and reputation. A Computer is powerful but the manipulator is the one who controls the powers. The powers that these home arrest cannot take away. If anything, the lockdown just gives you peace. Peace to build this template and host it online.
